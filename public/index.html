About HimalDoc

The Himalayan Document Centre (HIMALDOC) provides bibliographic information about different resources related to sustainable mountain development, as well as direct access to selected full-text and multimedia files in electronic format. The resources include books, articles, periodicals, theses, multimedia products, and other reference materials. The main collection consists of resources maintained at ICIMOD.
